NHA TRANG 해외여행

Nha Trang is a coastal city and the administrative center of Khanh Hoa province , Vietnam

Previously, Nha Trang land belonged to Champa , so Cham relics still exist in many places in Nha Trang.

Nha Trang was recognized as a class I urban area by the Prime Minister of Vietnam on April 22, 2009.

Nha Trang is known as the pearl of the East Sea , the Green Pearl [ 4 ] because of its natural value, beauty and climate. [ 5 ]
